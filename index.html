— Peter Löwenbräu Griffin, Sr.

Born Justin Peter Griffin, is a man of Irish descent currently residing in Quahog, Rhode Island with his wife Lois Griffin. Peter's favorite pastime is watching TV, with his favorite shows consisting of Star Trek, Three's Company and Charles in Charge. He is also a huge KISS fan and followed them during the KISS Stock shows and even got a copy of Kiss Saves Santa for Christmas. Peter is also an ardent fan of Barry Manilow, though this is a fact he has been known to deny in public. Peter enjoys the following breakfast cereals: Total, Trix, and Boo Berry. His favorite song is the Trashmen's "Surfin' Bird".
